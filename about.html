The Market Guru HP Story

Market Guru HP started in Vadodara with a clear mission: to bring trust and transparency to Gujarat's pre-owned car market. Hiren Patel (popularly known as HP Bhai) built a massive social media following by being radically honest about cars — reviewing every detail on camera, calling out meter tampering, accident history, and hidden flaws.

Today, we're a full-fledged digital marketplace connecting buyers with dealers and private sellers across Vadodara, Ahmedabad, Surat, Rajkot, and all of Gujarat. We are not a car dealer. We are a platform — a mediator — between HP Bhai's trusted audience and Gujarat's best car sellers.
